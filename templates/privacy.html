Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

BRAINIAC Downloader ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal productivity tool that lets registered users download YouTube videos and playlists. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data: your email address and hashed password when you register.
  • Google account: if you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your Google email and a public profile identifier. We do not store your Google password.
  • Google Drive: if you connect Google Drive, we store a temporary access token in your server session to upload files on your behalf. This token is never written to disk or shared.
  • Download history: URLs you submit and the resulting files. Files are stored temporarily and may be deleted automatically.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To process download requests you initiate.
  • To upload files to your Google Drive when you explicitly request it.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising.

4. Google Drive Access

When you connect Google Drive, this app requests the drive.file scope — which only allows access to files that the app itself creates. We cannot read, modify, or delete any of your existing Drive files. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions page or by clicking "Disconnect" in the app settings.

5. Data Retention

Downloaded files are stored temporarily on our server and deleted after a short period. Account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We use HTTPS for all traffic. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. OAuth tokens are stored only in server-side sessions.

7. Contact

For any privacy concerns, contact us at brainiactechhub@gmail.com.
